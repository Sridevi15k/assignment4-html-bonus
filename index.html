Multi Grain Crepe

Ingredients:

  • Urad dal: 1 cup
  • Brown Rice: 2 cups
  • Fenugreek Seeds: 1/2 tsp
  • Tur dal, millets, green gram dal, quinoa, Black eyed beans, Sago, Flat rice(poha), Chana dal: Fistful each
  • Salt
  • Water

Procedure:

Put all the above ingredients in a bowl, wash them and soak for 4 to 6 hrs. Grind it into a paste with little water. Let it ferment for a couple of hours, add enough salt and refrigerate it. When making a crepe mix the batter with some water, put a scoop on the crepe pan, spread it thin and add some oil. Let it cook until golden brown, flip it and serve with chutney.

Coconut Veggie Rice

Ingredients:

  • Basmati Rice: 1 cup
  • Mixed Veggies(Carrot, Corn, Lima Beans, Mint leaves, Onion, Spinach): 2 cups
  • Green Chillies: 2
  • Coconut Milk: 1 cup
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste: 1tsp
  • Water: 1 cup
  • Oil: 3TBSP
  • Butter: 1 TBSP
  • Whole Spices(Cardamom, Cloves, Cinnamon, Bay Leaf, Black Pepper, Star Anise): 1 each

Procedure:

In a pan, add oil and butter. Saute whole spices, add mixed veggies, salt and ginger-garlic paste. Saute until it absorbs moisture. Now add rice, fry on a low flame for 5 to 6 min. Mix coconut milk and water, pour into rice, adjust salt. Cover with a lid under medium flame and cook for 10 min.

Avocado Roti

Ingredients:

  • Whole wheat flour: 2 cups
  • Avocado mash: 3 TBSP
  • Clarified Butter: 1 TBSP
  • Warm Water

Procedure:

Mix flour, salt, avocado mash and clarified butter. Then add little by little warm water to make it into a dough that is of enough consistency to make patties. Cover and keep it aside for 15 to 20 min. Roll the lemon sized balls into tortillas with a rolling pin, put them one after another on a hot pan and spread little butter on both the sides. These goes well with Paneer Tikka Masala.

Paneer Tikka Masala

Ingredients:

  • Paneer cubes: 2 cups
  • Onion: 1 medium size
  • Tomato: 1 big
  • Ginger-Garlic: 1 tsp
  • Masala: 1/2 tsp
  • Cream: 2 TBSP
  • Oil

Procedure:

Shallow fry Paneer cubes in oil until light brown and keep them aside in little milk. In a little oil add chopped onion, tomato, green chillies, saute until transparent. Add salt, turmeric and chilli powder. Make it into a paste. Now put little oil in a pan, add onion tomato paste and ginger-garlic paste. Saute for 5 to 8 min. Add paneer along with milk, masala, cream. Cook on a low flame for 5 to 10 min. Serve hot with Avocado Roti.

Tandoori Chicken

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Drumsticks: 6 no.
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste: 1 tsp
  • Tandoori Garam Masala: 1 tsp
  • Yogurt: 1 TBSP
  • Lime juice: 1 TBSP
  • Dried methi leaves: 1 tsp
  • Red Chilli Powder: 1/2 tsp

Procedure:

Make a paste of ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, garam masala, yogurt, dried methi leaves, salt and red chilli powder. Slit the chicken drumsticks with a knife each with 3 to 4. Marinate the chicken with the above paste, let it sit in the refrigerator for few hours. Place the on a grill and let it cook for 15 to 20 min. Serve with onion, and salad on the side.

Roasted Salmon

Ingredients:

  • Salmon pieces: 2 to 4
  • Masala Powder: 1/2 tsp
  • Lime Juice: 1 TBSP
  • Ajwain seeds: 1/2 TBSP
  • Red Chilli Powder: 1/2 tsp
  • Dried Methi Leaves: 1 tsp
  • Yogurt: 1 TBSP

Procedure:

Make a paste out of ginger-garlic paste, lime juice, masala powder, yogurt, dried methi leaves, salt, ajwain seeds, and red chilli powder. Marinate Salmon with the above paste and let it sit overnight in the refrigerator. Roast them on a pan for 12 to 15 min flipping sides. Roasted Salmon goes well with salad or rice with tomato rasam(soup).

Crab Curry

Ingredients:

  • Crab: 2 pounds
  • Diced Onion: 1
  • Diced Tomato: 1 1/2
  • Shredded Coconut: 1/2 cup
  • Black pepper: 1/2 tsp
  • Fennel seed: 1/2 tsp
  • Ginger-Garlic: 1 tsp
  • Oil

Procedure:

Saute black pepper, fennel seeds in oil, then add onion and green chillies. Saute until translucent. Add tomatoes, salt, turmeric, red chilli powder and little water. Cook for 5 to 7 min. Add coconut and grind it into a paste. Add oil to the pan, put the onion tomato paste, saute for 2 to 3 min. Then add ginger-garlic paste, coriander powder, masala powder, water and cook until it boils. Then add crab, cook for 12 to 15 min. Serve hot with rice after adding curry leaves.

Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients:

  • Pasta of desired shape: 4 cups
  • Water
  • All purpose flour: 2 TBSP
  • Sliced Garlic: 1 TBSP
  • Butter: 2 TBSP
  • Onion: 1
  • Capsicum: 1
  • Milk: 1 cup
  • Parmesan cheese: 2 to 3 TBSP

Procedure:

Boil pasta in a big pot with 3 to 4 quart water. Meanwhile add butter to the pan, roast garlic, add all purpose flour, pepper powder, salt and slowly add milk on a low flame. Let it come to a smooth creamy consistency and then add grated parmesan cheese. In another pan add oil, saute sliced garlic, onion and capsicum. Then add cooked pasta. After 3 to 5 min, slowly add alfredo sauce on a low flame. Serve hot with Tandoori Chicken.

Egg Cubes Curry

Ingredients:

  • Eggs: 6 to 8
  • Onion: 1
  • Ginger-garlic: 1 tsp
  • Masala powder
  • Coriander Powder
  • Green Chillies: 2
  • Oil: 2 TBSP

Procedure:

To a little oil in a pan, add cumin, sliced onion and green chillies. Add salt and turmeric, saute until onions are transparent. Add red chilli powder and grind the sauteed onions into paste. In a separate pan, crack the eggs, add a tsp of onion paste, 1 tsp oil, 1 tsp milk, 1/4 tsp masala powder, mix well until soft and fluffy. Cook in the boilin water bath for 20 to 25 min. Cut it into cubes. Fry the onion paste in a pan, add ginger-garlic paste and add egg cube. Add masala and coriander powder, red chilli powder, curry leaves and corinader leaves. Serve hot with rice of stuff in a burrito.

Butter Cookies

Ingredients:

  • All Purpose Flour: 4 cups
  • Almond Flour: 1 1/2 cup
  • Powdered Sugar: 2 1/4 cups
  • Clarified Butter: 2 cups
  • Baking Soda: 1 tsp

Procedure:

Mix all the ingredients except clarified butter, then add warm clarified butter. Make into lime sized balls or use cookie cutters to get the desired shape. Put them on a baking pan. Bake at 325 F for 12 to 15 min.

Cake

Ingredients:

  • All purpose flour: 1 cup
  • Butter: 1 cup
  • Eggs: 1 cup
  • Baking soda: a pinch
  • Sugar: 1 cup
  • Vanilla Essence: 1/4 tsp

Procedure:

Beat the eggs with a hand mixer until fluffy, add sugar, butter and beat for 2 to 3 min. Add flour, vanilla Essence and baking soda and beat until smooth consistency. Pour in a cake pan, put it in the oven at 375 F for 12 to 15min.
